A recipe for hearty vegan pancakes

Ingredients

Dry

  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tbsp corn starch
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Flax seed, jams (optional)

Wet

  • 1 cup water or soy milk
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • 2.5 tbsp oil
  • 2.5 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Combine dry ingredients in a large, pyrex measuring cup.
  2. Add wet ingredients and whisk with a spoon until all clumps are gone. Make sure to scrape the bottom.
  3. Heat up an oiled, cast-iron pan to medium-high heat, and dole pancake mixure onto pan with a spoon. Size is up to you, but I've had good luck with 3 inch cakes.
  4. When the edges start to brown up and curl, it's time to flip.
  5. Another few minutes, and you're good to serve them. Make sure to have some decent VT syrup, NH will also do.
